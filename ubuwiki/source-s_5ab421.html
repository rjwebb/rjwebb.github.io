Claims

  • c_5ab421_0001 All predication is an assault upon the freedom of man.
    ALL PREDICATION IS AN ASSAULT UPON THE FREEDOM OF MAN
  • c_5ab421_0002 Poetry, as de Vree conceives of it, is no longer the handmaiden of princes, prelates, politicians, parties, or even the people.
    POETRY, AS I CONCEIVE OF IT, IS NO LONGER THE HANDMAIDEN OF PRINCES, PRELATES, POLITICIANS, PARTIES, OR EVEN THE PEOPLE
  • c_5ab421_0003 Poetry is a phonetic phenomenon in itself vocal of psychophysical origin.
    A PHONETIC PHENOMENON IN ITSELF VOCAL OF PSYCHOPHYSICAL ORIGIN
  • c_5ab421_0004 Poetry is objectively structured with the help of words, sounds and mechanical and graphic means (recordings and scripts).
    OBJECTIVELY STRUCTURED WITH THE HELP OF WORDS, SOUNDS AND MECHANICAL AND GRAPHIC MEANS (RECORDINGS AND SCRIPTS)

    Cited by: Kurt Schwitters and Dada Poetics

  • c_5ab421_0005 The purely visual verbal does not exist.
    THE PURELY VISUAL VERBAL DOES NOT EXIST
  • c_5ab421_0006 The purely visual verbal always rouses the sound or noise from which it springs and for which it is the sign.
    IT ROUSES ALWAYS THE SOUND OR NOISE FROM WHENCE IT SPRINGS AND FOR WHICH IT IS THE SIGN
  • c_5ab421_0007 The poem is either an audible emission of respiration (audition) or a silent one (reading).
    THE POEM IS EITHER AN AUDIBLE EMISSION OF RESPIRATION (AUDITION) OR A SILENT ONE (READING)
  • c_5ab421_0008 The poem is creatively adulated.
    CREATIVELY ADULATED
  • c_5ab421_0009 The poem is provoked by the need to say something.
    PROVOKED BY THE NEED TO SAY SOMETHING
  • c_5ab421_0010 The poem refers to nothing other than the sensibility of being (present and planetary).
    IT REFERS TO NOTHING OTHER THAN THE SENSIBILITY OF BEING (PRESENT AND PLANETARY)
  • c_5ab421_0011 De Vree understands the objective intention of vocal sonorities as a communication in concert of spontaneously creative vibrations.
    THIS IS WHAT I UNDERSTAND AS THE OBJECTIVE INTENTION OF VOCAL SONORITIES: A COMMUNICATION IN CONCERT OF SPONTANEOUSLY CREATIVE VIBRATIONS
  • c_5ab421_0012 Phonetic poetry cannot exist without a reinvention of the recitation, that is to say the sonorization or the manipulation of sound.
    PHONETIC POETRY CANNOT EXIST WITHOUT A REINVENTION OF THE RECITATION, THAT IS TO SAY THE SONORIZATION OR THE MANIPULATION OF SOUND
  • c_5ab421_0013 All depends upon the new possibilities of mechanical expression for realization of the transmission of the total sensibility of the poem.
    ACTUALLY ALL DEPENDS UPON THE NEW POSSIBILITIES OF MECHANICAL EXPRESSION FOR REALIZATION OF THE TRANSMISSION OF THE TOTAL SENSIBILITY OF THE POEM
  • c_5ab421_0014 The poem is itself at bottom part of the total kinetic spectacle.
    ITSELF AT BOTTOM PART OF THE TOTAL KINETIC SPECTACLE
  • c_5ab421_0015 Henri Chopin provides the total kinetic spectacle through the inevitable use of the machine.
    WHICH HENRI CHOPIN PROVIDES THROUGH THE INEVITABLE USE OF THE MACHINE
  • c_5ab421_0016 The machine breaks the voice up into waves.
    BREAKS THE VOICE UP INTO WAVES
  • c_5ab421_0017 The sound work is the result of teamwork under the direction of the poet.
    THE SOUND WORK IS THE RESULT OF TEAMWORK UNDER THE DIRECTION OF THE POET
  • c_5ab421_0018 The ideal reproduction is that which is cut on high-fidelity records.
    THE IDEAL REPRODUCTION IS THAT WHICH IS CUT ON HIGH-FIDELITY RECORDS
  • c_5ab421_0019 The machine is indispensable for the ideal reproduction of sound works.
    THERE AGAIN THE MACHINE IS INDISPENSABLE
  • c_5ab421_0020 The recitor (if it is not the poet) and the engineer of sounds (where de Vree's recordings are concerned) have contributed personally to the originality of the realization.
    THE RECITOR (IF IT IS NOT THE POET) AND THE ENGINEER OF SOUNDS (WHERE MY RECORDINGS ARE CONCERNED) HAVE CONTRIBUTED PERSONALLY TO THE ORIGINALITY OF THE REALIZATION
  • c_5ab421_0021 The dawn of the era of electronic poetry is no longer a figment of the imagination.
    THE DAWN OF THE ERA OF ELECTRONIC POETRY IS NO LONGER A FIGMENT OF THE IMAGINATION
  • c_5ab421_0022 Paul de Vree is from Belgium.
    Paul de Vree, Belgium
