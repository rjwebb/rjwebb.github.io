Christof Migone

Christof Migone is the author associated with La première phrase et le dernier mot, a 2004 book published by Le Quartanier.

La première phrase et le dernier mot

Migone’s book presents a structure organized around sourced first sentences and last words. Its material connects the opening and closing elements of texts with questions also addressed in Brennan, Mallarmé, and the Livre Composé and Mallarmé and Concrete Poetry.
