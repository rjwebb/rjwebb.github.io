Para-Poetry

Para-poetry is any use of language that accidentally or unconsciously employs words or sentences in interesting and unexpected arrangements.【c_15538e_0004】

Concept

In para-poetic use, people are not using language poetically; rather, language is using them para-poetically.【c_15538e_0017】

Related poetic practices

Concrete poetry introduces space into the ideogram as a substantive element of poetic structure.【c_07485b_0006】 It creates a new rhythmic, spatial-temporal reality.【c_07485b_0007】

In the account of Mallarmé and concrete poetry, Un Coup de Dés is the first poetic work consciously and structurally organized according to space-temporality.【c_07485b_0015】
