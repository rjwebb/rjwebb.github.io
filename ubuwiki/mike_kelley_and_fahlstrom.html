Framing

This page is framed around an interview between Sérgio Bessa and Mike Kelley concerning Öyvind Fahlström’s influence on Kelley’s art practice; the framing treats that influence as a significant inter-art connection.

Concrete poetry

Concrete poetry goes beyond the application of the ideogrammic process as practiced by Ezra Pound.[^c_07485b_0005] It introduces space into the ideogram as a substantive element of poetic structure and creates a new rhythmic, spatial-temporal reality.[^c_07485b_0006][^c_07485b_0007] In doing so, it destroys traditional linear rhythm.[^c_07485b_0008]

In Joycean terms, concrete poetry results from the interaction of the verbal, the ineluctable modality of the visible, and the ineluctable modality of the audible.[^c_07485b_0009] Mallarmé’s Un Coup de Dés is described as the first poetic work consciously and structurally organized according to space-temporality.[^c_07485b_0014][^c_07485b_0015] Its realization uses page space as an element of structure, with words in different typefaces and cases and with reading indications, making the page a verbivocovisual score.[^c_07485b_0017]

Intermedial poetic practice

Contamination always permeates Fontana’s poetic work.[^c_028d86_0001] The confluences of several artistic disciplines and the intersections of their languages distinguish that work.[^c_028d86_0002][^c_028d86_0003] Fontana aims at poly-dimensionality rather than at the mere sum of separate elements.[^c_028d86_0004] Body, gesture, noises, sound, lights, colours, and architectures play inter-linguistic roles in the poetic game.[^c_028d86_0005]

Fontana’s project is founded on a close interaction between vocalism and writing, creating continuously transforming areas of intervention.[^c_028d86_0010][^c_028d86_0011] Electronics occupies all but negligible spaces in these areas of intervention.[^c_028d86_0012] His writing produces pre-texts rather than texts; these pre-texts are places for acoustic and visual transfiguration and territories of action that can be reframed beyond the page in spatio-temporal terms.[^c_028d86_0013][^c_028d86_0014][^c_028d86_0015] They generate writings in motion and poetic performances capable of regenerating writings.[^c_028d86_0016][^c_028d86_0017]

The poet consequently transforms into a poly-artist who takes possession of electronic, videographic, cinematic, photographic, sonorous, theatrical, and rhythmic universes.[^c_028d86_0035] The poly-artist acts poetically through all techniques, supports, and spaces without renouncing the body, gesture, or voice.[^c_028d86_0036] This poetry creates tensions through the contamination of systems, the penetration of separate universes, and the use of new media and supports.[^c_028d86_0038]
