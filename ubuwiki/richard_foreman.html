Richard Foreman

Richard Foreman is a theater practitioner whose work involves compositional method, poetics of production, and theater practice.

Compositional method

Foreman’s compositional method includes generating texts. His theater practice is associated with the Ontological-Hysteric Theater.

Theory

Foreman’s theory includes an isomorphism between art and feeling. His work is also referenced in relation to performance and the poetics of presence.
