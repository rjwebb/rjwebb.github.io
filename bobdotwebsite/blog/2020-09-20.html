tennis

"Almost anyone can learn to play a game of tennis in just a single afternoon if they don't try too hard"

This video is incredible. Skip to 1:39 if you just want to see the clip where the action happens. Timothy Gallwey, the author of "The Inner Game of Tennis" teaches a lady with no prior knowledge of tennis how to play in twenty minutes.

After the clip, Alan Kay mentions some key points of "The Inner Game of Tennis". I won't go over them here, but it's worth watching the video to learn about them. These principles could easily apply to other difficult activities. Mr Gallwey is well aware of this - the "Inner Game of..." series includes books on Golf, Work, Music and Stress.
